Product Redesign · 11 screens

ADHDAPT — the calmer system

Every screen below carries the Focus Fest DNA — playful rounded cards, the pink / teal / cream / blue palette, Outfit + Playfair Display type, hard offset shadows — but gently softened for non-game surfaces: thinner borders, softer shadows, more breathing room. Game & Community keep the arcade energy; Mood, Journal & Learn go noticeably serene. Each screen is shown desktop + mobile. All animated icons are drawn as labeled static placeholders so nothing loops.

Understand your brain

ADHD, explained kindly

It's not a deficit of attention — it's difficulty regulating it. Here's what's actually going on, and gentle strategies that work with your wiring, not against it.

Inattentive

Daydreamy, easily sidetracked, loses track of details. Often missed because it's quiet, not disruptive.

Hyperactive

Restless, always "on," acts before thinking. A body and mind that struggle to sit still.

Combined

Both patterns together — the most common presentation. Different days can look different.

Coping strategies that actually stick

Body-double it

Work alongside someone (even virtually) to borrow their focus.

Shrink the first step

Make it laughably small: "open the doc" beats "write report."

Make time visible

Visual timers turn abstract minutes into something you can see.

Drop the shame

Self-criticism burns the fuel you need. Kindness is a strategy.
